Discovery Brief

Community Management Service

Please answer the questions below — the more detail you provide, the more accurately we can build your vision. There are no right or wrong answers, just write what feels true to you.

01 — Product Vision
Q1
Describe your service in one sentence.
Explain it the way you'd tell a friend — no jargon, just the core idea.
Q2
What core problem does this service solve?
What pain does your audience have today, and how does your product fix it?
Q3
Who is your target customer?
e.g. Community managers, startup founders, HR teams, Discord server owners, brand teams, etc.
Q4
Are there any competitors or similar products you've already looked at?
What do you like about them? What would you do differently or better?
02 — Platforms & Integrations
Q5
Which platforms should the service support?
Telegram, Discord, Slack, WhatsApp, Reddit, Circle, custom forums — list everything relevant.
Q6
Do you need integrations with external tools?
CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce), analytics (Mixpanel, Amplitude), email platforms, payment providers, Notion, Jira, Zapier, etc.
Q7
Is this a web app, mobile app, or both?
Any platform preferences for the service itself (not the communities it manages)?
03 — Features & Functionality
Q8
What features are absolutely must-haves for launch?
Moderation tools, activity analytics, role management, automation, member onboarding, content scheduling, reporting — what can't you ship without?
Q9
Do you need automation — bots, triggers, auto-responses?
Give examples of what should happen automatically without human involvement.
Q10
Should AI be part of the product?
e.g. AI-powered moderation, sentiment analysis, auto-generated replies, conversation summaries, member insights.
Q11
What metrics and reports need to be visible in the analytics dashboard?
Member growth/churn, engagement rate, top contributors, conversion, geographic data, message volume — what do you need to track?
Q12
Do you need a roles and permissions system inside the service?
Who will use the platform — just you, a team, or multiple client organizations? What access levels are needed?
04 — Business Model
Q13
How will the service be monetized?
Subscription (SaaS), one-time payment, freemium, usage-based pricing, enterprise licensing — or something else?
Q14
Are you planning multiple pricing tiers?
If yes — describe how they differ. If no — what's included in the single plan?
Q15
Do you need a customer dashboard with billing and subscription management?
Or will this be handled manually or through a third-party service like Stripe Billing?
05 — Scale & Constraints
Q16
How many users / communities do you expect at launch and at scale?
This helps us design the right architecture and infrastructure from day one.
Q17
Are there any security or data compliance requirements?
GDPR, SOC 2, data residency (US/EU), end-to-end encryption, SSO, 2FA, HIPAA, etc.
Q18
What is your approximate budget and target timeline?
Even a rough range helps us prioritize features and define a realistic MVP scope.
06 — Design & UX
Q19
Do you have any preferences for the interface style or feel?
Minimal, corporate, modern, dark mode, a specific reference — e.g. "like Linear", "like Notion", "like Stripe Dashboard".
Q20
Do you already have branding — logo, colors, typography?
If yes, please share files or describe the style. If not — do you need branding developed as part of this project?
07 — Wrap-Up
Q21
What does a successful launch look like to you?
What outcome 3–6 months after launch would make you genuinely proud of this product?
Q22
Is there anything important we haven't asked that you'd like to add?
Any ideas, concerns, non-negotiables, or context that would help us understand your vision better.
